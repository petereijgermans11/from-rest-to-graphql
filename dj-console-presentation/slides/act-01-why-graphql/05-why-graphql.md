# What if the client could ask what it needs?

<p class="fragment fade-in" data-fragment-index="1">
Not a URL. Not a fixed document.<br>
A <strong>query</strong> — authored by the client.
</p>

<p class="fragment fade-in" data-fragment-index="2" style="margin-top:0.6em;">
The client declares exactly what it needs.<br>
The server answers <strong>precisely that</strong>.
</p>

<p class="fragment fade-in" data-fragment-index="3" style="margin-top:0.8em;">
Same endpoint.<br>
Same schema.<br>
<strong>Different shape every time.</strong>
</p>
